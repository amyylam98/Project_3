Pokemon Game Information

Pokemon X

Release Date: October 12, 2013
Developer: Game Freak
Director(s): Junichi Masuda
Publisher(s): The Pokemon Company, Nintendo
Platform(s): Nintendo 3DS
Pokemon Generation (Core Series): Generation VI

Official Description:

When Pokemon fans begin their thrilling 3D adventure in Pokemon X or Pokemon Y this October, they will be transported into an entirely new region called Kalos. A mysterious place that is shaped like a star, Kalos is a region where players will encounter beautiful forests, thriving cities, and many never-before-seen Pokemon. The central city of this breathtaking region is Lumiose City, a metropolis featuring a tower that is set to become an iconic structure in Pokemon X and Pokemon Y.
