Pokemon Game Information

Pokemon Ruby

Release Date: March 19, 2003
Developer: Game Freak
Director(s): Satoshi Tajiri, Junichi Masuda
Publisher(s): The Pokemon Company, Nintendo
Platform(s): Game Boy Advance
Pokemon Generation (Core Series): Generation III

Official Description:

Start your adventure on Pokemon Ruby and receive one of 3 basic Pokemon from the professor. Journey through Hoenn gaining knowledge and training your Pokemon and earning badges with the goal of becoming a Pokemon Master through collecting, battling & trading.

Pokemon Emerald

Release Date: May 1, 2005
Developer: Game Freak
Director(s): Shigeki Morimoto
Publisher(s): The Pokemon Company, Nintendo
Platform(s): Game Boy Advance
Pokemon Generation (Core Series): Generation III

Official Description:

Emerald takes place over land and sea and pits you against the Team Aqua and Team Magma from Saphire and Ruby. This time around, Pokemon will show off their moves prior to battle, perhaps as a sort of taunt. Nintendo also added some new options to the game. A new Battle Frontier area has you play through seven big battles. The Battle Dome places you in a tournament in which only three Pokemon can participate. The Battle Tube has you select from three entrances and attempt to make your way to a goal.
