Pokemon Game Information

Pokemon Ultra Sun

Release Date: November 17, 2017
Developer: Game Freak
Director(s): Kazumasa Iwao
Publisher(s): The Pokemon Company, Nintendo
Platform(s): Nintendo 3DS
Pokemon Generation (Core Series): Generation VII

Official Description:

A new light shines on the Alola region! Take on the role of a Pokemon Trainer and unravel the mystery of the Legendary Pokemon Necrozma's new forms: Dusk Mane Necrozma in the Pokemon Ultra Sun game and Dawn Wings Necrozma in the Pokemon Ultra Moon game. You can encounter and battle 400+ Pokemon, including new forms. A brand-new showdown awaits!
